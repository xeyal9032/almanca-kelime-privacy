Almanca Kelime iOS Application

OstWind Group ("we", "us", "our") respects the privacy of users of the Almanca Kelime application ("App").

Data Collection

The Almanca Kelime app does not collect user data.

The App:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not collect device information
  • Does not send data to third-party services
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not use analytics services

Local Data Storage

The App only stores data locally on your device:

  • Your learning progress (UserDefaults)
  • Favorite words and sentences
  • Quiz results
  • Statistics

This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for ages 4+ and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
